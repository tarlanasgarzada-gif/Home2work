About Homework App

Homework App helps students organize assignments across multiple subjects, manage timelines and due dates, and receive reminders so they start and finish work on time.

This app centralizes homework for many subjects into a clear schedule. Students can add assignments with subject tags, set start and due dates, and view timelines that highlight upcoming tasks. Each assignment supports detailed notes, priority levels, and estimated effort so planning is straightforward.

Timelines and calendar views show deadlines and suggested work windows. The app sends automated reminders before start times and due dates to keep students on track, with configurable notifications so users receive alerts when they need them most.

Use the Homework App to break large projects into manageable steps, see all subjects at a glance, and build better time management habits that ensure punctuality and consistent progress.
